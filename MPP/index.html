What I do

Web Development Services

As a web developer, I offer a range of services to help businesses and individuals create and maintain their online presence. My services include custom website design, responsive design, and website maintenance. I have experience working with a variety of technologies, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and am committed to delivering high-quality, user-friendly websites that meet the needs of my clients.

Responsive Design

I understand the importance of creating websites that are mobile-friendly and easy to use on all devices. That's why I specialize in responsive design, which ensures that your website looks and functions great on any device, from desktop computers to smartphones. I use a variety of techniques, including media queries and flexible grids, to create websites that are easy to navigate and provide a great user experience on all devices.

Website Maintenance

I offer ongoing website maintenance services to help ensure that your website stays up-to-date and secure. This includes tasks like software updates, backups, and security checks. I also offer add-on services like content updates and website redesigns to help keep your website fresh and engaging for your users.

Custom Web Applications

In addition to standard websites, I also have experience in developing custom web applications to meet the specific needs of my clients. Whether you need a simple application to streamline your business processes or a more complex system to manage and track data, I have the skills and experience to help you achieve your goals. I have experience with a variety of technologies, including PHP and MySQL, and can help you to choose the best solution for your needs.
