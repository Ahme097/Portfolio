About AI Resume Assistant

AI Resume Assistant is a website that uses artificial intelligence to help job seekers create and optimize their resumes. With a simple and user-friendly interface, the site makes it easy for anyone to create a professional and competitive resume in just a few clicks.

Features

  • AI-powered resume optimization and formatting
  • Customizable templates
  • Personalized job recommendations
  • One-on-one career coaching
  • Resume review and feedback from industry experts

Pricing

AI Resume Assistant offers both a free version and a subscription-based premium version. The free version includes basic resume templates and optimization tools, as well as cover letter creation tools and basic resume statistics. The premium version includes additional features such as customized job recommendations and one-on-one coaching from career experts.

Support

AI Resume Assistant is committed to providing excellent customer support to all of our users. Whether you have a question about using the site or need help with a specific issue, our team is here to help.
